The problem

Small businesses are drowning in manual work.

For many local businesses, the biggest problem is not lack of demand. It is operational overload. Business owners spend hours every day answering WhatsApp messages, confirming appointments, handling cancellations, repeating prices, updating spreadsheets, checking payments and trying to understand their own numbers.

Every unanswered message can become a lost customer. Every missed follow-up can become lost revenue. Every manual process becomes a hidden cost.

Message

Customers expect instant replies

But business owners cannot stay on the phone all day.

Calendar

Manual scheduling creates mistakes

Appointments, confirmations and cancellations quickly become hard to manage.

Database

Business data is scattered

Client history, service records, payments and insights are often split between WhatsApp, calendars and spreadsheets.

Chart

Financial visibility comes too late

Many small businesses only understand what happened after the month is already over.

The solution

Meet Sofia, the first LABORYA AI Worker.

Sofia is an AI business assistant designed to work inside the channels small businesses already use — starting with WhatsApp.

She talks to customers naturally, answers questions, helps with bookings, confirms appointments, sends reminders, supports post-service workflows and records operational data that can become business intelligence.

Sofia is not a generic chatbot. She is a specialized digital worker designed for real business routines.

How it works

From conversation to execution.

LABORYA combines conversational AI, workflow automation and business context to create digital workers that can execute real tasks. Sofia connects customer conversations, calendars, forms, spreadsheets, automations and business rules into one operational flow.
